Learn Abhidhamma

http://abhidhamma.com

Ven Ayya Agganyāni from Bruckmuehl, Germany, is the designer, manager, and editor of this website. As a dedicated nun, her goal is to introduce and promote Abhidhamma as a systematic and practical exposition of the Buddha-Dhamma, which is an integral part of the Pāli Canon. The website provides comprehensive foundational knowledge, translations, and information in English, serving as a valuable resource for those interested in exploring Abhidhamma.
